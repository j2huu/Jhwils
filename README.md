## 🧠 Research & Deep Dive

- **RL-based Locomotion:** Exploring **Reinforcement Learning** (PPO/SAC) for quadrupedal robots and bipedal walking in constrained environments.
- **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models:** Investigating the integration of **Multimodal LLMs** (like RT-2 or OpenVLA) for high-level task planning and end-to-end robotic manipulation.
- **Sim-to-Real Transfer:** Utilizing **MuJoCo** and **Isaac Gym** for policy training and domain randomization to bridge the gap between simulation and hardware.
